COMPREHENSIVE SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on March 15, 2024, between Digital Solutions LLC ("Provider") and Enterprise Corp ("Client").

1. DEFINITIONS AND INTERPRETATION
For the purposes of this Agreement, the following terms shall have the meanings set forth below:
"Services" means the software development, consulting, and maintenance services described in Schedule A.
"Deliverables" means all work products, documents, and materials developed by Provider.
"Confidential Information" means any proprietary or confidential information disclosed by either party.

2. SCOPE OF SERVICES
Provider agrees to perform the following services:
a) Custom web application development using modern frameworks
b) Database design and optimization services
c) User interface and user experience design
d) Quality assurance testing and bug resolution
e) System integration and API development
f) Documentation and training materials preparation

3. PROJECT TIMELINE AND MILESTONES
The project shall be completed in the following phases:
Phase 1: Discovery and Planning (3 weeks) - Due April 5, 2024
Phase 2: Design and Architecture (4 weeks) - Due May 3, 2024
Phase 3: Development and Implementation (8 weeks) - Due June 28, 2024
Phase 4: Testing and Quality Assurance (2 weeks) - Due July 12, 2024
Phase 5: Deployment and Go-Live (1 week) - Due July 19, 2024

4. PAYMENT TERMS AND CONDITIONS
Total project cost is $125,000, payable as follows:
a) 25% upon execution of this Agreement ($31,250)
b) 25% upon completion of Phase 2 ($31,250)
c) 30% upon completion of Phase 3 ($37,500)
d) 20% upon final acceptance and delivery ($25,000)
All invoices are due within 30 days of receipt.
